Hello! This bot combines the convenience of *Telegram*, the power of *ChatGPT*, and the fun of *Tinder*.

*The chatbot can*:
1. Generate a Tinder profile based on your description 😎
2. Write interesting and intriguing opening messages 😘
3. Handle conversations on your behalf 😇🔥
4. Allow you to practice chatting with the bot 🥰

*Useful commands and links:*
/start — bot's main menu
/profile — generate a Tinder profile 😎
/opener — opening message for introductions 🥰
/message — handle conversations on your behalf 😈
/date — chat with celebrities 🔥
/gpt — ask any question to the GPT chat 🧠;
